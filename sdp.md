> Applications are typically processed within a few days, though processing times may be longer during peak periods, such as the start of a new semester.
> To ensure your account is verified by the first day of class, **please sign up a.s.a.p.**!

> Learn more about the Student Developer Pack [here]({% link student_dev_pkg.md %})

# How to sign up for Student Developer Pack?

1. **Access the application page:**  
   Visit [GitHub Student Developer Pack page](https://education.github.com/pack) and click on **Sign up for Student Developer Pack**

   <div style="width:70%">
   <img alt="Student Developer Pack Sign up page" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/sdp_1.png" title="Sign Up Page"/>
   </div>
2. **Sign in to your GitHub account**
   - Make sure you are signed in to an account that is linked with your university email.
3. Under **Select your role in education** select **Student**

   <div style="width:70%">
   <img alt="Select your role in education" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/sdp_2.png" title="Select your role"/>
   </div>
4. Scroll to the bottom of the page and fill out the **Application**.
   - Select or add your **school-issued email address**.
   - Specify your **school name**.

     <div style="width:80%">
     <img alt="Student Pack Verification Application" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/sdp_3.png" title="Student Pack Verification"/>
     </div>

   {: .note }
   > After clicking `Continue`, you will be prompted to share your location so GitHub can verify your affiliation with your chosen school.

   <div style="width:70%">
   <img alt="Share your location" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/sdp_4.png" title="Location share"/>
   </div>
5. **Upload proof of your academic status**  
   You will be prompted to upload proof of your academic status.
   - Under **Proof Type**, use the dropdown to select the type of proof you are providing.

     <div style="width:70%">
     <img alt="Take a Picture for Academic Proof" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/sdp_5.png" title="Upload Academic Proof"/>
     </div>
6. **Review and submit your application**
   - Verify all details in your application.
   - Click **Process my application** to submit.

   {: .note }
   > If you see a banner asking you to fix something in your application after clicking `Process my application`, follow the instructions to correct the issue and then click `Reprocess my application`.
7. **Wait for approval:**  
   Applications are usually processed within a few days, but it may take longer during peak times, such as during the start of a new semester.
   You’ll receive a confirmation email once your application is approved!
